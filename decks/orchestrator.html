Orchestrator Program

Humans, AI, and the Shift Beyond “Just a Worker”

The Orchestrator Program is a 4-hour, high-intensity workshop and toolkit that helps people move from AI anxiety and survival mode into a new identity as an Orchestrator: someone who can think with AI, design systems, and protect what actually matters in their work and life.

Format: 4-hour workshop + tools Core arc: Align → Disrupt → Reform → Assist Audience: L&D, knowledge workers, AI-curious teams
This hub collects the live tools participants use during and after the workshop.
