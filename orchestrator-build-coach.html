Build Your AI Coach

This page helps you define who your AI Coach is, how it should support you, and how it should understand your identity, context, and tools. When you're done, you'll copy a single configuration prompt into your Coach to give it a clear role.

You can keep this rough at first. Think of it as a Version 1 character sheet. You and your Coach will refine it together over time.

Already familiar with the tools? Visit the Muscle Toolkit or set up Drift & Reset next.
1. Who is your Coach?
Tone, style, role. How do you want this Coach to feel when you talk to it?
2. You: Role & Work Context
What do you do? What kinds of problems do you bring to the Coach?
3. Your Orchestrator Identity
How does your “Orchestrator self” think, act, and choose?
4. Boundaries & Red Lines
Topics or behaviors where the Coach should be careful, limited, or redirect to humans.
